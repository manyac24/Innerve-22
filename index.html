    INNERVE'22

    Innerve is the annual technical fest of Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women (IGDTUW), catering to the interests of numerous students across all fields. Experiencing a footfall of over 9000 students from all over the country every year, Innerve is a platform where technical acumen blends with creativity and innovation.

    This two day technical fest of IGDTUW, Delhi showcases a plethora of events ranging from coding competitions to overnight hackathons, from powerful debates to robots at war. The new edition of INNERVE is bigger, bolder and better than ever before. Come join us in our pursuit of infinity and beyond.
